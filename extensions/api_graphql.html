API:GraphQL

Bonfire is a federated and modular social network. It can be assembled and configured from the bottom-up to empower individuals and communities to design, operate and control their own digital lives. Built upon ActivityPub - a W3C standard protocol - every Bonfire instance can communicate with the rest of the so-called fediverse, a virtual decentralised social space where users of many different open source platforms can all interconnect.
