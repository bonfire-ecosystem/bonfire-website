bonfire

Your plug & play federated social network

Bonfire looks to boost the diversity and the ability to express ourself in social networks, putting your data in your own hands (literally), with the possibility to define rules and shape the user experiences of the tools we use, without loosing the capacity to connect with the people and communities we care of.

Receive updates about the crowdfunding

We want anyone be free to truly shape their social network experience, from the ground up.

bonfire is

A device

Plug & play

It is ready to use (really, plug in and play)

Online, offline or local network

It can be used offline or within your local network

Open source

It runs on the Raspberry Pi

No coding required

You can easily install/remove any CommonsPub apps and extensions

A fediverse hosted service

Always online

Hosts your fediverse identity, on your own domain name or using @your-instance.bonfire.cafe

Complements your Bonfire device

By collecting your incoming messages/updates until you come online, and then syncing them, as well as your outgoing activities.

Respects your privacy

We will implement end-to-end encryption so that the server can never see you data.

Doesn't hog your data

Once your private data (like DMs) has synced to your device, it gets deleted from the server, and you decide how much of your public data is kept.

Open source & decentralised

You can self-host it instead, or sign up to a compatible service
